# Business Funding Document Prep Kit

**Get Your Files Lender-Ready Before You Need Money**

---

## Product Overview

This comprehensive kit helps business owners organize their financial documents and prepare for funding applications. Most entrepreneurs wait until the last minute to scramble for documents—this system ensures you're always prepared and look organized and serious to potential lenders.

## File Directory

### Core Documents (Starter Version)

1. **README.md** — This document. Table of contents and master reference.

2. **funding-document-checklist.md** — Complete checklist of every document you'll need for a typical funding application. Organized by category with explanations of why each document matters.

3. **bank-statement-prep-sheet.md** — Guide for organizing and preparing your business bank statements. Includes tips for presenting the best picture of your business health.

4. **business-summary-template.md** — Professional business summary template that lenders love. Fill in your details to create a compelling one-page overview of your business.

5. **use-of-funds-worksheet.md** — Structured worksheet for explaining exactly how you'll use the funding. Lenders want to see clear thinking, not vague plans.

6. **revenue-snapshot-sheet.md** — Template for presenting your revenue history in a format that lenders can quickly assess and verify.

7. **debt-overview-worksheet.md** — Comprehensive debt inventory template. Shows lenders you understand your full financial picture.

8. **folder-organization-guide.md** — Visual guide for organizing your digital and physical files. Consistent organization makes you look professional and saves you time during applications.

9. **application-readiness-checklist.md** — Final checklist before submitting any funding application. Ensures nothing falls through the cracks.

---

## Reading Order Recommendation

1. Start with **funding-document-checklist.md** to understand what you're working toward.

2. Read **folder-organization-guide.md** to set up your organizational system.

3. Use **bank-statement-prep-sheet.md** to gather and prepare your statements.

4. Complete **business-summary-template.md** for your company overview.

5. Fill out **revenue-snapshot-sheet.md** and **debt-overview-worksheet.md** with your financial data.

6. Complete **use-of-funds-worksheet.md** to define your funding goals.

7. Run through **application-readiness-checklist.md** before any submission.
