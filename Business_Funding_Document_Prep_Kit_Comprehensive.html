BANK STATEMENT PREP SHEET

How to Organize and Present Your Business Bank Statements

What Lenders Are Actually Looking For
Five key evaluation factors
1. Average Daily Balance

Higher average balances suggest financial stability. They want to see balances that would cover your proposed monthly loan payment multiple times over.

2. Cash Flow Consistency

Consistent, regular deposits from customers suggest reliable revenue. Predictable deposit patterns that match your business model.

3. Expense Management

Large, unusual withdrawals or consistent overspending can signal poor financial management. Categorize your major expenses.

4. Balance Trends

Growing balances suggest a healthy, profitable business. Calculate your month-over-month balance change to highlight growth.

Email Scripts for Lender Communication
Professional templates for following up
Script 1: Initial Application Follow-Up

Subject: Application Follow-Up - [Company Name]

Dear [Lender Name],

I wanted to follow up on our recent application for [loan type] submitted on [date]. Since our submission, we've been preparing any additional documentation you might need to expedite the review process. Please let me know if there's anything else you require.

Best regards, [Your Name], [Title], [Phone]

Script 2: Status Check-In (Week 1)

Subject: Quick Check-In - [Company Name] Application

Dear [Lender Name],

I hope this message finds you well. I wanted to check in briefly on the status of our application submitted on [date]. We've been working to ensure all supporting documentation is current and readily available. Is there a good time for a brief call to discuss any questions?

Best regards, [Your Name], [Phone]

Script 3: Request for Feedback (After Denial)

Subject: Request for Application Feedback - [Company Name]

Dear [Lender Name],

Thank you for reviewing our application. While I understand the decision was not in our favor this time, I would greatly appreciate any feedback you could provide. Understanding the specific factors that influenced the decision would help us strengthen our application for future opportunities.
