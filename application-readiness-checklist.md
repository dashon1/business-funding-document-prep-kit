## Purpose of This Checklist

Use this checklist for every funding application, whether it's your first or your tenth.

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

**Mistake 1: Submitting outdated documents**
Lenders want statements from the last 30-90 days, not from six months ago.

**Mistake 2: Missing pages**
Bank statements often span multiple pages. Ensure every page is included.

**Mistake 3: Inconsistent business names**
Your legal name, DBA, and bank account name must align or be explained.

**Mistake 4: Forgetting personal guarantees**
If you personally guarantee the loan, include your personal financial documents.

**Mistake 5: Unclear use of funds**
"Lump sum" and "working capital" are vague. Be specific about exactly what the money will accomplish.

**Mistake 6: Math errors**
Double-check every calculation. Lenders will catch mistakes, and they raise questions about your attention to detail.

## Pro Tips for Successful Applications

1. **Submit complete packages** — Partial applications get delayed or rejected
2. **Tell a coherent story** — Your documents should tell the same story
3. **Be proactive** — If something looks unusual, explain it before they ask
4. **Follow instructions** — Lenders often have specific formatting requirements
5. **Stay organized** — Keep copies of everything you submit
6. **Follow up** — Call to confirm receipt and check on status
7. **Be responsive** — Answer questions quickly to keep the process moving

## If You're Denied

Don't give up. Request feedback on why you were denied and address those issues before applying elsewhere. Common reasons for denial:

- Insufficient revenue history
- Too much existing debt
- Poor credit scores
- Missing documentation
- Unclear use of funds

Work on these issues and try again. Many successful businesses were denied initially before finding the right lender.
