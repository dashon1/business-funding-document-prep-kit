# Debt Overview Worksheet

**Document Every Dollar You Owe—And Show Lenders You Understand Your Full Financial Picture**

---

## Why This Document Matters

Lenders need to understand your complete debt obligations before extending new credit. They calculate your debt service coverage ratio, evaluate your leverage, and assess risk based on your existing obligations. A comprehensive debt overview demonstrates financial transparency and shows lenders you manage your obligations responsibly.

This worksheet helps you document every debt, understand your payment obligations, and present your financial picture clearly.
