## Tips for Presenting Your Revenue

1. **Be consistent** — Use the same accounting method and time periods throughout
2. **Explain anomalies** — Note any unusual months with clear explanations
3. **Show trends** — Highlight growth patterns and stability
4. **Document everything** — Keep source documents organized and accessible
5. **Be conservative** — If projecting future revenue, use reasonable assumptions
6. **Address concentration** — Proactively explain customer dependencies
