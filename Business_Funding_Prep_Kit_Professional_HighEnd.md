## Why Bank Statements Matter to Lenders

Lenders analyze your bank statements to verify:

- **Cash Flow Consistency** — Regular deposits and stable balances demonstrate reliable income
- **Business Revenue Verification** — Deposits should align with reported income figures
- **Expense Management** — Spending patterns reveal operational efficiency
- **Cash Reserve Maintenance** — Adequate balances indicate ability to cover obligations
- **Payment History** — How you manage financial relationships with others
